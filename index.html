‘More Black Designers’
is the New Black


he 2024 McDonald’s Change of Fashion Program is
focused on driving change when it comes to Black
representation in fashion. The industry frequently
pulls inspiration from Black culture, but doesn’t always
embrace Black creatives. After all, only 7.3% of
American fashion designers are Black.*

So, we’re pairing five, rising Black designers with five
fashion insiders for career-changing mentorships. The
program will also provide access and resources, and
give their brands a chance to shine.

*Per Zippia.com as of 2021.
